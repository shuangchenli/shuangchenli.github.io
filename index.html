I'm now Research Scientist at Alibaba DAMO Academy, Sunnyvale. Previously I worked as a postdoc in SEALab, Dept. of ECE, UCSB under supervision of Prof. Yuan Xie, where I also received my Ph.D from UCSB in 2018, and M.S./B.S. from Tsinghua University.

My research interest resides at addressing the memory wall and the power wall challenges arisen by big data applications by designing memory-centric architectures. My research focuses the architecture design efforts of bridging the device and circuit level innovation with the application demands.
